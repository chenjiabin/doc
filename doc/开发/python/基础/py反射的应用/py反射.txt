__import__(str)：以字符串方式导入模块
getattr(mode_name,function_name)：以字符串方式调用函数


例：
In [2]: model = 'sys'
In [3]: sys1 = __import__(model)

In [4]: sys1.path
Out[4]: 
['',
 '/usr/local/python2.7/bin',
 '/root/.ipython']

In [5]: function = 'path'
In [6]: func_sys_path = getattr(sys1,function)

In [7]: func_sys_path
Out[7]: 
['',
 '/usr/local/python2.7/bin',
 '/root/.ipython']
